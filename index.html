Solution

The Connected Campus pushes the student to go beyond the LMS, beyond social media, and beyond the disconnect associated with learning today. Our solution transforms student engagement for 21st century millennials and non-traditional students by breaking down the barriers of accessibility while building loyalty to the institution. Through timely resource coaching and meaningful connections, your institution will achieve new heights of retention success. Go beyond.

ACADEMIC HALL

Go beyond the LMS in the Academic Hall. Designed to motivate and guide students to remain focused on their educational goals, the Academic Hall provides specific action items that push the student to persist with their dream of earning a degree. The Academic Hall integrates with the institution’s LMS and SIS platforms to smartly surface timely and personalized tasks and resources.

COURTYARD

Go beyond social media in the Courtyard where students develop connections to relevant topics, fellow classmates, and important staff and faculty members. As the ‘Halls and Walkways’ of the institution, the Courtyard enables a student’s personality to shine while fostering relationships between students with similar circumstances and challenges. In the Courtyard, a student is never alone when experiencing the vibrant culture of the institution and their journey of achieving a higher degree.

RESOURCE CENTER

Go beyond the disconnect and uncertainty of not know who to ask or where to go for important guidance in the Resource Center. The personal presentation of staff and faculty connections removes any doubt on where to go for help. With easy to navigate content the Resource Center ensures the student can overcome obstacles and receive support every step of the way.
