Providing a Student with the right information at the right time leads to a more engaged student; a more engaged student is more likely to persist.

The Resource Center consolidates resources from multiple systems into one easy to access menu. Through single-sign-on technology, students no longer have to remember where to go and how to log into their LMS, library, email, or payment center. Students are introduced to their dedicated staff and current faculty with personal bios and direct message access.
