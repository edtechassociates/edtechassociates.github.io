Students who stay current academically have higher confidence; academically confident students are more likely to persist.

In the Academic Hall, your students have all the information they need to plan and execute class work. Connected Campus provides each student with a personalized academic task list and a proactive notification system. Using data from your LMS and SIS, Connected Campus understands what a student needs to do to maintain momentum and creates an individualized daily task list. Students can access online class resources or campus personnel and offices by clicking on the task in the Timeline. With Academic Hall, the student maintains an organized and disciplined approach to their academic objectives resulting in persistence from day to day.
